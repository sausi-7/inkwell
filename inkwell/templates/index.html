How it works

  1. 1
    Teach your voice. Bio, tone, three or more real comments you've written. Takes ten minutes; pays back forever.
  2. 2
    Pick where to listen. Subreddits, keywords, thresholds. Start with five, not fifty — quality beats breadth.
  3. 3
    Scan — free. Rule-based scoring surfaces the Yes / Maybe / No signals with a transparent reason. Zero tokens. Scan as often as you like.
  4. 4
    Draft on demand. Click Draft on a signal. Your browser sends your key (never stored on the server); a voice reply comes back in seconds. Copy, edit, send — you stay in control.
